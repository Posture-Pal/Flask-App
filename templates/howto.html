Step 1: Get Your Device

First things first. You Will need a PosturePal Pack to track your posture and receieve useful insights on the app. You can get your pack here. Once you receive your Pack in the mail, unbox it and ensure it is connected to a power source such as a power bank.

Step 2: Create Your Free Account

Navigate to PosturePal and download the site as a PWA to always have it as a mobile app. Alternatively, you can use PosturePal as a website. Whichever works for you! Login with Google for your convenience and you will be welcomed into our app.

Step 3: Wear Your Device

If it is your first time using the app you will be prompted to calibrate your posture. Wear the device on your back and follow the instructions on screen. Get into your ideal posture and press continue. The device will alert you if you slouch too far from this posture.

Step 4: Complete!

You have completed the setup process. You can now continue to monitor your posture progress using the dashboard on the home page and view your past data on the statistics page. Make sure to toggle your preferred alert method in the settings. Feel better, look better, be better!
